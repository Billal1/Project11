About Us

We are a family operated restaurant offering a variety of home cooked Greek Dishes in a very
classy, yet laid back environment. We believe that life has to be enjoyed, by you, your family,
and friends alike. And we do our best to accommodate this by providing exceptional food at
a reasonable price. So whether you come with your family, friends, come for business or alone we invite you to
join us for a fabulous meal and experience. From our family to yours, here is to great food, conversation, health and a life full of happiness! Yasou!
